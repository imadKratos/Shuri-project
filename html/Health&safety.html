Our Coaches

Our coach operators are well established and reliable, whether chosen by us or supplied by trusted agents. They provide a high standard of vehicles and drivers, which are both trustworthy and safe, and compliant with local regulations. Seat belts on coaches are available in most Rwanda country.

Travel Insurance

Travel Insurance for your group is compulsory. We offer very competitive group rates for schools. Should you decide to organise it directly with another insurer, we will require copy of your insurance certificate at least 2 months prior to departure.
