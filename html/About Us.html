Why are airfares not included in the quote?

Teachers/organisers are entitled to cost visibility and clarity. To that end, SHURI provides a cost for the land content alone.

We give you the option to shop around for flights and source airfares from your local retail agent. We can also provide flights to match your tour requirements with the best value and least travel disruption.

The choice is yours!
Baggage Allowance on airlines
Remember that an international flight includes one 20kg checked luggage item per person (except for music bands which usually have extra allowance for musical instruments). Please check your quotation carefully. Any additional luggage is charged by the airline at check in.
Financial security

SHURI is fully insured with QBE Business Insurance including cover for money paid by our clients. A Certificate of Currency can be obtained from QBE at any time. School payments are received directly into our Trust Account.

School groups must have travel insurance and if your school insurance does not cover your group, we can provide Group Travel Cover through QBE-NIB travel insurance.
